Silk Icons

http://www.famfamfam.com/lab/icons/silk/

“Silk” is a smooth icon set, containing over 700 16-by-16 pixel icons in strokably-soft PNG format. Containing a large variety of icons, you're sure to find something that tickles your fancy. And all for a low low price of $0.00. You can't say fairer than that.

Current version: 1.2. New icons may be added occaisionally. If you can think of an icon that would make the set even better, send me an email with a description and I'll see what I can do. Likewise, if you make any derivatives or icons you feel would fit with the set, whisk them into an email (they will be added to an additional download with your name credited, and released under the same license).
